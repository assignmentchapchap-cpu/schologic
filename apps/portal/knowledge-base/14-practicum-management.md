# Practicum Management

## Overview

The Practicum Management module enables students to log their industrial attachment or teaching practice activities and allows instructors to verify these entries efficiently. This digital logbook replaces traditional physical logbooks, ensuring real-time tracking and easier assessment.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Digital Logbook

Students can log their daily activities, including tasks performed, skills learned, and challenges faced. The system supports both daily logging and weekly composite logs depending on the institution's requirements.

**Key Features:**
- **Daily & Weekly Modes**: Flexible logging frequency.
- **Structured Entries**: specific fields for "Teaching Practice" (e.g., Topic, Observations) vs. "Industrial Attachment" (e.g., Department, Skills).
- **Time Tracking**: Log Clock In and Clock Out times.

**How It Benefits You:**
- Eliminate physical logbook loss.
- Monitor student progress in real-time.
- Standardized data for easier grading.

---

### 2. Final Practicum Report

At the end of the attachment, students can upload their final report (PDF/Word) directly through the portal for grading.

**Key Features:**
- **File Upload**: Supports PDF and Word documents.
- **Version Control**: Students can re-upload corrections if needed (before grading).
- **Rubric Grading**: Instructors grade reports using a digital score sheet.

**How It Benefits You:**
- Centralized storage of all student reports.
- Integrated plagiarism checks (via AI detection).
- Streamlined grading process.

---

### 3. Instructor Verification

Instructors (University Supervisors) can review student logs, verify them, or request changes.

**Key Features:**
- **Verification Dashboard**: View pending logs in a structured timeline.
- **One-Click Verify/Reject**: Efficiently approve or send back entries.
- **Feedback Loop**: Provide comments when rejecting entries.

**How It Benefits You:**
- Reduce administrative burden during assessment.
- Provide timely feedback to students.
- Track attendance and engagement remotely.

## Student Workflows

### How to Log an Activity

1. **Access the Practicum Module**
   - Navigate to **Practicum** from the main dashboard.
   - Select your active practicum placement.

2. **Create a New Entry**
   - Click the **+ New Entry** button.
   - Select the **Date** for the activity.

3. **Fill in Details**
   - **Time**: Enter Clock In and Clock Out times.
   - **Activity**: Describe tasks performed, skills acquired, and any challenges.
   - *For Teaching Practice*: Enter Subject, Class, and Lesson Topic.

4. **Submit**
   - Click **Save & Exit** to save as a draft.
   - Click **Submit** when ready for instructor review.

### How to Submit Final Report

1. **Go to 'Final Report' Tab**
   - In your practicum dashboard, click the **Final Report** tab.
   
2. **Upload Document**
   - Click to browse or drag-and-drop your report file (PDF/DOCX).
   - Wait for the upload to complete.

3. **Verify Submission**
   - Ensure the status changes to "Report Submitted".
   - You can view the uploaded file to check formatting.

### How to View Status

- **Pending**: Waiting for supervisor review.
- **Verified**: Approved by the supervisor (Green checkmark).
- **Rejected**: Returned for corrections (Red cross). Check comments for details.

---

## Instructor Workflows

### How to Verify Logs

1. **Open Student Logbook**
   - Navigate to the **Class** or **Practicum Group**.
   - Select a student to view their logbook.

2. **Review Entries**
   - Click on a "Pending" entry to view details.
   - Review the tasks, time, and reflection.

3. **Action**
   - **Verify**: Marks the day/week as approved.
   - **Reject**: Marks as invalid. You must provide a reason (e.g., "Insufficient detail").

## Frequently Asked Questions

| Question | Answer |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Can I edit a log after submission?** | No, unless the instructor rejects it. |
| **What if I missed a day?** | You can log past dates if the week is not yet locked. |
| **Who approves my logs?** | Your assigned University Supervisor or Workplace Supervisor. |
